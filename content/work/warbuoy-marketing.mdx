## Project Overview

Warbuoy Marketing is a business growth marketing agency website offering complete business growth solutions including profitable ads, AI automation, CRM management, and web development. The site positions Warbuoy as a new agency with a data-driven, transparent approach.

## The Challenge

The agency needed a website that:
- **Demonstrates Fresh Perspective**: Show they're not just another agency
- **Builds Trust**: Transparent processes and clear value proposition
- **Showcases Four Services**: Clearly explain ads, automation, CRM, and web development
- **Generates Qualified Leads**: Convert visitors into strategy calls
- **Performs Fast**: Fast loading times for better UX and SEO

## My Approach

I built a conversion-focused website that positions Warbuoy as a modern, data-driven agency with:

1. **Clear Value Proposition**: "Complete business growth solutions" with four core services
2. **Service Showcase**: Dedicated sections for each service area
3. **Transparent Process**: Four-step process clearly explained
4. **Trust Signals**: 100% transparent processes, 24/7 support, 30-day trial
5. **Performance Optimized**: Lighthouse score of 98 for speed and SEO

## Tech Stack

- **Next.js 15**: Server-side rendering for SEO and performance
- **TypeScript**: Type-safe development
- **Tailwind CSS**: Rapid UI development with consistent design
- **Framer Motion**: Smooth animations that enhance UX
- **Vercel**: Edge deployment for global performance

Built to handle high traffic and convert visitors at every stage of their journey.
