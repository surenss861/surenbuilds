## Project Overview

Scandish is an instant QR menu platform that lets restaurants create and update digital menus in seconds without PDFs or designers. Customers simply scan a QR code to view the menu on their phone—no app download required.

## The Problem

Restaurants were struggling with:
- **Expensive Printing Costs**: Constantly reprinting menus is costly
- **Update Difficulty**: Changing prices or specials requires full reprints
- **Time Delays**: Can't respond quickly to ingredient availability or market changes
- **No Analytics**: No visibility into what customers are viewing
- **Contact Concerns**: Shared physical menus raise hygiene concerns

Restaurants needed a solution that was fast to set up, easy to update, and saved money on printing.

## My Solution

Scandish solves these problems with a simple promise: **Edit menus in seconds, no PDFs or designers needed.**

- **Next.js 15**: Fast, SEO-friendly pages for each menu
- **TypeScript**: Type-safe development
- **Tailwind CSS**: Beautiful, responsive design
